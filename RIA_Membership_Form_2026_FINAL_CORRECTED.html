8 BUSA Committee Participation

As an ARIA member, you may participate in Business Unity South Africa (BUSA) structures through ARIA's membership. BUSA is the principal representative of organised business in South Africa and the main avenue through which business communicates, develops policy positions, and influences government.

Each member may nominate one principal representative and one alternate to each structure. ARIA will coordinate the formal BUSA nomination process on your behalf. This section captures your participation interests for ARIA's records.

Committee and External Representation Participation ARIA welcomes member participation in its committees and in external bodies such as BUSA and the BBC. Participation enriches our collective voice and strengthens the industry's representation at the highest levels.
  1. Submission of a nomination is the first step in the participation process. Appointments are confirmed by ARIA following an assessment of available positions, member standing, and nominee suitability.
  2. ARIA coordinates nominations to external bodies on behalf of its members. Placement is subject to the rules and availability of the relevant external body and is not guaranteed.
  3. Participation is open to members who are current on their membership obligations. ARIA may review participation where a member's standing changes.
  4. Where a member representative participates in an external body through ARIA's membership, they are expected to represent the broader interests of the rail industry and to keep ARIA informed of relevant developments.
  5. ARIA may from time to time review committee composition to ensure broad and effective industry representation.
  6. Personal information submitted in this section will be used solely for membership administration and representation coordination, in accordance with POPIA.
  7. These terms are read alongside the ARIA Memorandum of Incorporation.
